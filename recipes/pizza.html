Pizza

Description

This recipe will introduce you to the best type of pizza, Detroit style deep dish with old world style pepperonis. An all around favorite dish of the mid michigan families

The staples of this dish are the crispy and crunchy corners and the soft bready internals under a thin layer of cheese and sauce topped with old world style pepperonis, these curl up and get crispy on the edges adding a satisfying crunch and almost burnt taste.

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • bread flour
  • instant yeast
  • salt
  • water
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Add the Flour, Salt, and Yeast to a bowl then stir to combine
  2. Slowly add water while stirring until the dough comes together in a rough ball
  3. Let stand for 10 minutes or until fully risen
  4. Press into rectangualr baking sheet

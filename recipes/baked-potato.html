Baked Potatoes

Description

A very simple recipe that goes well with nearly all dinner dishes.

If you have a potato, an oven/microwave and some salt and butter you have the perfect baked potato.

Ingredients

  • potato
  • salt
  • butter

Steps

  1. Poke holes into the top of the potato
  2. Cook until soft
  3. Cut down the top center and split, add a pad of butter and some salt
  4. Close the potato and wait for it to cool
